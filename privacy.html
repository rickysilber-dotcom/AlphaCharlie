Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Your data stays on your device

Alpha Charlie does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. Everything stays on your iPhone.

Alpha Charlie is a match analytics app for USPSA and IDPA competitive shooters. We take privacy seriously. This policy explains what data the app uses and how it is handled.

Data Collection

What we collect

Alpha Charlie does not collect any personal information. The app does not require an account, does not connect to any server, and does not transmit any data over the internet.

The only data the app stores is:

  • Your shooter name, which you optionally enter in Settings and is stored only on your device
  • Match results data that you capture from publicly available match results pages
  • App preferences such as theme and font size settings

All of this data is stored exclusively in local device storage (UserDefaults) and never leaves your device.

If you purchase a subscription, all payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. Alpha Charlie never sees, receives, or stores your payment information. Purchase receipts are verified directly with Apple's StoreKit framework on your device.

Third Parties

Third party sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. There are no analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, or tracking libraries in Alpha Charlie.

The app uses Google Fonts (Barlow) loaded from Google's CDN for typography within the dashboard interface. This is a standard web font request with no personal data attached.

Permissions

Device permissions

Alpha Charlie does not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, or location.

The app requests access to your Photo Library only when you choose to save a match result share card to your device. This permission is requested at the moment you tap save and is never used for any other purpose.

The app uses an in-app browser to navigate to match results websites. No browsing history is stored by the app.

Your Rights

Deleting your data

You have full control over your data at all times. You can delete all stored match data at any time using Settings → Clear All Data within the app. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data permanently.

Contact

Questions

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please reach out via the Support page.
