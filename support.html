Getting Started

How to capture a match

1
Go to Settings and enter your name exactly as it appears in match results.
2
Tap the + button on the Matches tab to open the in-app browser.
3
Navigate to your match results page. The app will detect and import your results automatically.
4
Tap Done when the capture completes to return to the dashboard.
Common Questions

FAQ

Why can't I find myself in the results?

Make sure your name in Settings matches exactly how it appears on the results page — spelling, spacing, and capitalization all matter.

The app says "capture failed" — what do I do?

Navigate directly to the results page for your specific match (not the homepage) and wait for it to fully load before the app can detect the data.

Can I use Alpha Charlie for both USPSA and IDPA?

Yes — use the USPSA / IDPA toggle in the header to switch between sports. Each sport has its own match history and dashboard.

How do I back up my match data?

Go to Settings → Backup to export all your data to a file. You can save it to iCloud, Files, or share it anywhere. Use Restore to reload it.

Does Alpha Charlie work with classifier results?

Alpha Charlie captures any match result page — classifiers, local matches, and major matches are all supported as long as results are posted online.

Is my data private?

Yes — all data is stored only on your device. Nothing is sent to any server. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
